P2P [Peer To Peer Reward System]

Project Description

This system was designed with the intentions of using it as company wide reward system for employees to reward peers for doing activites that support the companies values. Employee A may have donated blood at the company blood drive and Employee B wants to recognize that employee. Using our system Employee B can securly log in to their personal account reward the desired employee (Employee B) and give reasoning for the reward. Employee B will recieve the points in real time and then be able to exchange points for monetary rewardss.

This system is currently under development with an expected completion date of late April.

Development Enviroment

C#, ASP.NET, Microsoft SQL Server

Project Details

  • Tablue Analytics Integration
  • CEO and Employee accounts for easy management
