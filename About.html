Who Am I?

I am a geek at heart. I enjoy "nerding" out over feats of architecture, mid-century modern furniture, cars, and mechanical keyboards. I like to watch Formula 1, work on my latest woodworking project, and take nice long drives in my Miata. I am always up for a trip to the nearest coffee shop to talk about board games or French culture.

In August of 2017 I married my highschool sweetheart Faith Ripa. After two weeks in Nashville, Sparta, and Piegion Forge we came back home to Harrisonburg Virginia so that I could finish out my senior year at James Madison University. After about a month of marriage we decided that something was missing and we needed a pet cat. We found our son at a local organization called cats craddle and the rest was history. Our family of three is always looking for our next adventure and always looking to take naps on the weekend.
